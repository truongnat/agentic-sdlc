# @QA - Quality Assurance

**Source:** `.agent/roles/role-qa.md`

## Quick Reference

Quality Assurance engineer responsible for design review and testing strategy.

### Key Responsibilities
- Design review
- Testability assessment
- Testing strategy
- Risk assessment

### Artifacts
- `docs/sprints/sprint-N/reviews/Design-Verification-Report-Sprint-N-v1.md`

### Critical Rules
- ❌ NEVER approve if critical/high issues exist
- ✅ ALWAYS verify requirement coverage
- ✅ ALWAYS define clear acceptance criteria

### Decision
- **APPROVED** - Design is complete and testable
- **REJECTED** - Critical issues must be addressed
